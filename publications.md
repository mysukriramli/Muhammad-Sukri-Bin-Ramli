## 🟢 The "Pattern Recognition" Series: Mineral Intelligence & Supply Chains
This series utilizes advanced machine learning architectures (Deep Autoencoders, Network Graph Analysis, Holt-Winters Time-Series Forecasting) to map structural volatility, arbitrage, and illicit transshipments in global commodity flows.

* **Pattern Recognition of Critical Mineral Copper in Global Trade Data** (April 2026)  
    * **Key Finding:** Introduces the *Apex Empirical Model* and *Stage-Specific Starvation (SSS)*. Quantifies a 38.2% empirical circularity discount for copper scrap and an arbitrage spread averaging $12.17/kg, alongside the *Circular Risk Parity (CRP)* portfolio allocation.
    * **Repositories:** [Cambridge Open Engage (ID: 69d789a6d1922e37d54dd529)](https://www.cambridge.org/engage/coe/article-details/69d789a6d1922e37d54dd529) | [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/403693778_Pattern_Recognition_of_Critical_Mineral_Copper_in_Global_Trade_Data)
* **Pattern Recognition of Gold and Mercury Supply Chain in Global Trade Data** (2026)  
    * **Key Finding:** Proposes a 'Mineral Intelligence' pipeline using Gaussian Mixture Models (GMM) to identify a systemic 'Balloon Effect', revealing illicit mercury disguised as 'fake waste' following 2022 international bans.
    * **Repositories:** [Cambridge Open Engage](https://client.prod.orp.cambridge.org) | [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net)
* **Pattern Recognition of Artificial Intelligence Hardware in Global Trade Data** (2026)  
    * **Key Finding:** Deploys a proprietary *Physicality Correlation benchmark* to audit semiconductor trade (HS 8542 and HS 8419), separating tangible technological ecosystem development from transactional capital flight.
    * **Repositories:** [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/scientific-contributions/Muhammad-Sukri-Bin-Ramli-2343553309) | [SSRN](https://papers.ssrn.com)
* **Pattern Recognition of Aluminium Arbitrage in Global Trade Data** (2025)  
    * **Repositories:** [arXiv:2512.14410](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.14410) | [SocArXiv](https://osf.io/preprints/socarxiv)

## 🔵 The "Pattern Recognition" Series: Environmental Treaties & Illicit Trade
Focused on detecting misclassification, evasion, and compliance with international environmental treaties (like the Basel Convention and Montreal Protocol) using UN Comtrade ledgers.

* **Pattern Recognition of Ozone-Depleting Substance Exports in Global Trade Data** (Dec 2025)  
    * **Key Finding:** Introduces a novel machine learning framework to systematically monitor the Montreal Protocol, isolating suspicious ozone-depleting substance (ODS) trade patterns directly from customs datasets.
    * **Repositories:** [arXiv:2512.07864](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.07864) | [Cambridge Open Engage](https://client.prod.orp.cambridge.org)
* **Pattern Recognition of Illicit E-Waste Misclassification in Global Trade Data** (2025)  
    * **Key Finding:** Formulates an interpretable AI framework to expose a novel "inverse price-volume signature"—a systemic anomaly where reported volumes artificially inflate as average unit prices decrease.
    * **Repositories:** [arXiv:2509.21395](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.21395) | [Cambridge Open Engage](https://www.cambridge.org)
* **Pattern Recognition of Scrap Plastic Misclassification in Global Trade Data** (2025)  
    * **Key Finding:** Applies Random Forest models to UN Comtrade HS 39 data, effectively flagging high-risk environmental smuggling segments with 93.75% accuracy.
    * **Repositories:** [arXiv:2511.08638](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.08638)

## 🟣 AI Customs Enforcement, Economics & Public Policy
Research mapping the socio-economic impacts of automated systems, macroeconomic policy, and the optimization of public sector operations.

* **Examining the Impact of Income Inequality and Gender on School Completion in Malaysia: A Machine Learning Approach Utilizing Malaysia's Public Sector Open Data** (Jan 2025)

    * **Repositories:** [arXiv:2501.18868](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.18868)
