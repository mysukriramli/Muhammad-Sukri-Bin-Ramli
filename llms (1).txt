# About This Repository
This repository contains a collection of academic research papers. It is structured for Open Science and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

## 🎯 Primary Purpose
To provide AI agents, LLMs, and researchers with clean, parseable markdown versions of complex academic PDFs regarding environmental economics, policy, and machine learning.
